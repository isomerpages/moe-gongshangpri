<p><strong>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <u>National Anthem and National Pledge</u></strong>
</p>
<p>1.1&nbsp;&nbsp; Students who are <strong>Singapore Citizens </strong>must
sing the National Anthem and take the National Pledge. They are to stand
at attention, sing the national anthem and recite the pledge (<strong>with their right fist placed over their heart</strong>)
clearly during the flag-raising ceremony.</p>
<p>1.2&nbsp;&nbsp; Students who are non-Singapore Citizens must stand at
attention and show respect to Singapore during the flag-raising ceremony.</p>
<p><strong>2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <u>Attendance and Punctuality</u></strong>
</p>
<p>2.1&nbsp; Punctuality and attendance for all school activities is compulsory.
Absence from school or prescribed school activities must be substantiated
with a Medical Certificate or a Letter of Excuse from the student’s parent.
The school will accept a maximum of five Letters of Excuse from parents
per child, per semester, for one day of absence each time. Only Medical
Certificates will be accepted to substantiate a student’s absence from
a school-based assessment (e.g. weighted assessment, examinations etc).</p>
<p>2.2&nbsp; Students <strong>must report to the assembly venue in school (e.g. school hall / classrooms) by 7.30 am each school day</strong>.
Students who fail to do so will be considered late for school.</p>
