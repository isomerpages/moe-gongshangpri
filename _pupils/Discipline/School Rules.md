<h2><strong>School Rules</strong></h2>
<h4><strong>1. <u>National Anthem and National Pledge</u></strong></h4>
<p>1.1&nbsp;&nbsp; Students who are <strong>Singapore Citizens </strong>must
sing the National Anthem and take the National Pledge. They are to stand
at attention, sing the national anthem and recite the pledge (<strong>with their right fist placed over their heart</strong>)
clearly during the flag-raising ceremony.</p>
<p>1.2&nbsp;&nbsp; Students who are non-Singapore Citizens must stand at
attention and show respect to Singapore during the flag-raising ceremony.</p>
<h4><strong>2. <u>Attendance and Punctuality</u></strong></h4>
<p>2.1&nbsp; Punctuality and attendance for all school activities is compulsory.
Absence from school or prescribed school activities must be substantiated
with a Medical Certificate or a Letter of Excuse from the student’s parent.
The school will accept a maximum of five Letters of Excuse from parents
per child, per semester, for one day of absence each time. Only Medical
Certificates will be accepted to substantiate a student’s absence from
a school-based assessment (e.g. weighted assessment, examinations etc).</p>
<p>2.2&nbsp; Students <strong>must report to the assembly venue in school (e.g. school hall / classrooms) by 7.30 am each school day</strong>.
Students who fail to do so will be considered late for school.</p>
<p>2.3&nbsp;&nbsp;Students must engage in Silent Reading when they have reported
to the assembly venue (e.g. school hall / classroom), until the flag-raising
ceremony commences at 7.30 am.</p>
<p>2.4&nbsp; During school hours <strong>(including lunch breaks before remedial, CCAs and all afternoon activities)</strong>,
students are not allowed to leave the school without permission from the
School Leaders or leave school unaccompanied by a parent / guardian.</p>
<p>2.5&nbsp;&nbsp;Students who are not feeling well and wish to go home earlier
than the official dismissal time must obtain approval from the class teacher.
A parent / guardian must fill up the ’Permission to Leave Early Form’ in
the General Office before the student can leave school with the parent
/ guardian.</p>
<p>2.6&nbsp;&nbsp;All Primary 1 and Primary 2 students are not allowed to
leave school unaccompanied after school dismissal.</p>
<h4><strong><u>3. Personal Appearance</u></strong></h4>
<p>3.1&nbsp; Pupils are to be properly attired at all times.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Wear the prescribed school uniform at all times when they are in school
(including school holidays and for all school activities). Please refer
to the images below for an illustration of how the prescribed school uniform
should be worn. <strong>Modifications to the prescribed school uniform are not allowed.</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Wear the prescribed PE attire only on days when there are PE lessons.
House or CCA T-shirts can only be worn on Wednesdays.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>School shirts (for boys) and all school-based t-shirts are to be neatly
tucked in at all times.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Wear white socks and white shoes with no designs or logos. Socks should
cover the balls of the ankles.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>3.2&nbsp; <u>Students’ hair must be kept neat and tidy at all times. No tinted,</u>  <u>dyed, messy or fanciful hairstyles are allowed. Please refer to</u>  <u>the photos for acceptable and appropriate hairstyles.</u>
</p>

<h4><strong><u>4. Personal Conduct</u></strong></h4>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong>4.1&nbsp;&nbsp; Students are to show respect for
the school by singing the School Song with pride, and by not bringing disrepute
to the school during and after school hours, both in and out of school.</p>
<p>&nbsp;4.2&nbsp;&nbsp; Students are responsible for their own behaviour
and learning by:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Being attentive during lesson or activities,</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Listening and complying with all given instructions and school rules carefully
and fully,</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Completing all given work by themselves (i.e. not copying from others)
neatly and diligently,</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Submitting all given work and assignments punctually</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Being honest during assessments (e.g. test, examination, checking of scripts,
etc) by not copying from others or making unapproved alterations on assessment
scripts and official documents.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>4.3&nbsp; Students are to show good behaviour by always maintaining self-discipline
during and outside school hours both in and out of school by showing respect
for themselves and others through:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Being respectful, sensitive, considerate, and responsible when interacting
with other pupils, teachers and school staff – face-to-face, online and
through all forms of communication channels.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Respecting and taking care of all school or public facilities and property
by not damaging, removing or vandalizing them.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>4.4&nbsp; Students are to <u>observe Safety Rules at all times.</u>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students are not to cause hurt, discomfort or pose a threat to others
(i.e. <strong>Strictly No Bullying </strong>– in thoughts, words or actions
– face-to-face, online and through all forms of communication channels).
Dangerous and hurtful or harmful behaviour (both actions and words) is
not allowed and will not be tolerated. Such behaviour should be reported
to school staff immediately.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students must move quietly and in an orderly manner within the school
premises. Unsupervised and reckless running within the school ground is
not allowed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students can only run or engage in recreational play (with school equipment
unless otherwise instructed by teachers) in the parade square area.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are not allowed to play outdoors when the Lightning Warning System
has been activated or as instructed by the school.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are not allowed to remain in the classroom, along the corridors
and staircases during recess, lunch breaks or after school except when
accompanied by a teacher.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students <strong>MUST </strong>follow traffic rules and <strong>always observe road safety.</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>4.5 Students are to keep the school clean at all times.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students are only allowed to consume food and drinks within the canteen.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are to return all used cutlery, plates and cups to the respective
food stalls after their meals.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are to ensure that the tables, benches and areas that they have
used are clean before they leave the canteen. d.Students are to dispose
all litter in the bins provided and keep every part of the school clean.
During snack breaks, pupils should dispose all unconsumed food and food-related
litter into the bins outside their classrooms.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>4.6&nbsp;Students are to be responsible for the safekeeping of their personal
valuables (e.g. wallets) and belongings by taking good care of them at
all times (i.e. not leaving them unattended at any time).</p>
<h4><strong><u>5 Mobile phones / Smartwatches and Othe Unauthorised</u> <u>Devices</u></strong></h4>
<p>5.1 Students are not to use mobile phones and/or wear smartwatches except
the POSB Smart Buddy watch, during school hours including recess, CCA and
after-school programmes (e.g. remedial / supplementary / enrichment etc).</p>
<p>5.2 Students who bring such devices to school are to switch them off and
put them in their lockers or their school bags before school starts, and
retrieve them only after school ends. Students are to be responsible for
the safekeeping and use of these devices (in accordance with the school
rules) if they want to bring such items to school. The use of POSB Smart
Buddy watches is allowed throughout the school day.</p>
<p>5.3 After school hours, students are not allowed to use mobile phones
and/or smartwatches(except the POSB Smart Buddy watch) within the school
compound, except at the main / side gates or car porch after school, while
waiting for their parents to fetch them.</p>
<p>&nbsp;5.4 Students are not allowed to bring any toys, card games, scissors
and unauthorised electronic / communication / entertainment / gaming devices
capable of capturing, storing, displaying and / or transmitting visual,
audio or verbal information to others, to school. This does not apply to
the POSB Smart Buddy watch.</p>
<h4><strong><u>6 Prohibited Items &amp; Involvement / Behaviour</u></strong></h4>
<p>6.1 All students are not allowed to have in their possession any prohibited
items / weapons / weapon-like items which can be used, or intended to be
used, to cause harm to others.</p>
<p>6.2&nbsp;&nbsp; Students are not allowed to possess any improper or unlawful
material. Students are not to behave inappropriately in school or in public.</p>
<p>6.3&nbsp; Students are not to be involved in smoking / vaping / any type
of gang activity. Any student found participating in such activities will
be referred to the authorities accordingly.</p>
