### **Reading Star 阅读之星**

<img src="/images/chi1.jpg" style="width:85%">

阅读之星－每个学段末，老师会选出阅读最多华文书籍的学生成为阅读之星。这是第一学段的阅读之星。

Reading Star – at the end of each term, teachers will select the pupil who read the most number of Chinese books.
