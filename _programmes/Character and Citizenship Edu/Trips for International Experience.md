<h3><strong>Trips for International Experience (TIE)</strong></h3>
<p>The TIE programme aims to support schools in designing internationalisation
experiences that align with the refreshed objectives, focusing on destinations
in Asia, particularly in ASEAN countries, China and India Develop in our
students the 21CC of global awareness and cross-cultural skills and sensitivities.</p>
<p>&nbsp;The TIE programme is designed for internationalisation experiences
in alignment with MOE’s Learn for Life movement and Future of Learning
vision. These experiences can enhance students’ development of Emerging
21st Century Competencies (E21CC), specifically in global and cross-cultural
literacy.</p>
<p>&nbsp;The objectives of the TIE programme are to:</p>
<p>&nbsp;a. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Deepen in our
students the Emerging 21st Century Competencies (E21CC) of civic, global,
and cross-cultural literacy;</p>
<p>b. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Expose students
to Asia with a greater focus on ASEAN countries, China and India, and cultivate
an appreciation of the region’s cultures, growing economic opportunities
and dynamism; and</p>
<p>c. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Develop students’
awareness of our contemporary realities, to strengthen their confidence
and commitment to Singapore.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Since 2024, the school has organised overseas trips to Vietnam for selected
P5 students. The choice of Vietnam as the TIE destination country for GSPS
TIE include the following:</p>
<p>&nbsp;1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Historical,
Cultural and Economic Significance</p>
<p>2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Relevance to
P5 Social Studies that include topics like ASEAN and UNESCO Heritage Sites.</p>
<p>To achieve the objectives of the programme, TIE trips typically include
the following: tour to historical sites, visit to a partner school, visit
to agricultural villages, watch cultural performances, learn local crafts
and experience local cuisine.</p>
