<h4><strong>Raising a Happy, Confident, and Kind Generation Together</strong></h4>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/2024 uploads/guideschoolhomeelab.png">
</div>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<table style="minWidth: 50px">

<col>
</colgroup>
<tbody>
<tr>
<th rowspan="1" colspan="2">
<p>Our children do best when schools and parents work hand-in-hand to support
them:</p>
</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>Work with the school to know and support your child</strong>
</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Have regular conversations with teachers in both academic and non-academic
areas — this will help you better guide your child’s development.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Check the teachers’ preferred mode of communication — they are not required
to share their mobile numbers.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Understand that teachers may not be able to respond to your queries immediately.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>Help your child develop good habits</strong>
</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Create a conducive home environment for your child to enjoy reading and
to finish his/her homework by himself/herself.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Encourage your child to live healthy, balanced life with time for sleep
and play.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>Help your child relate to others</strong>
</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Work with the teachers to help your child respect differences and resolve
disagreements amicably.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Let the school verify the facts in any incident before taking action to
ensure a fair, objective outcome for all parties.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>Help your child manage himself/herself</strong>
</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Guide your child in managing his/her time.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Encourage your child to help out with household chores.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
