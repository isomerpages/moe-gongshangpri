<h4>School Assessment Policy</h4>
<p>a.&nbsp; Absence from assessments must be covered by a medical certificate
issued by a doctor.</p>
<p>b.&nbsp; Students with valid medical certificates will receive a 'VR'
(Valid Reason) for the missed assessment. His / her overall marks for that
subject will be computed based on his / her achievement in the other assessments.</p>
<p>c.&nbsp; Parental letters of excuse will not be accepted as valid reasons
for absence.&nbsp;</p>
<p>d.&nbsp; Students who fail to provide a valid medical certificate will
receive zero marks for the missed assessment.</p>
<p>e.&nbsp; For the well-being of your child/ward, parents/guardians are
advised not to send your child/ward to school on the day of the assessment
if he/she is unwell.</p>
<p>f.&nbsp; Students exhibiting any of the following conditions on the day
of assessment will be sent home to rest and not be allowed to take the
assessment:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>•&nbsp; Fever</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>•&nbsp; Flu-like symptoms</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>•&nbsp; Communicable diseases (e.g. HFMD, chickenpox, gastroenteritis)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>•&nbsp; On medical leave</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>g.&nbsp; Alternative arrangements will be made for students who are selected
to participate in National School Games (NSG) or Singapore Youth Festival
(SYF) competitions to take the assessment at a different time, should the
competition dates coincide with the assessment dates.</p>
