<h2><strong>School Assessment Policy</strong></h2>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Absence from assessments must be covered by a medical certificate issued
by a doctor.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students with valid medical certificates will receive a 'VR' (Valid Reason)
for the missed assessment. His / her overall marks for that subject will
be computed based on his / her achievement in the other assessments.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Parental letters of excuse will not be accepted as valid reasons for absence.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students who fail to provide a valid medical certificate will receive
zero marks for the missed assessment.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For the well-being of your child/ward, parents/guardians are advised not
to send your child/ward to school on the day of the assessment if he/she
is unwell.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students exhibiting any of the following conditions on the day of assessment
will be sent home to rest and not be allowed to take the assessment:</p>
<p></p>
<p>• Fever</p>
<p>• Flu-like symptoms</p>
<p>• Communicable diseases (e.g. HFMD, chickenpox, gastroenteritis)</p>
<p>• On medical leave</p>
<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Alternative arrangements will be made for students who are selected to
participate in National School Games (NSG) or Singapore Youth Festival
(SYF) competitions to take the assessment at a different time, should the
competition dates coincide with the assessment dates.</p>
</li>
</ol>
