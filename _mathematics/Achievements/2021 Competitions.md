### **2021 Competitions**
#### **Singapore Math Kangaroo Contest 2021**

Â Singapore Math Kangaroo Competition (SMKC) is a unique competition, and it is different from the other math competitions. Its syllabus is less advanced than other Olympiad competitions, and students can compete without any special training or advanced knowledge.

SMKC questions are set during Annual Math Kangaroo Conference by 150 educators from 70 countries. With all that math brainpower focused on creating interesting problems, SMKC has the best math problems in the world for all students to enjoy solving.
