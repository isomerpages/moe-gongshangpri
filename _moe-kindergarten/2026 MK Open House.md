<p>We’re excited to welcome you to our Open House 2026!</p>
<p>&nbsp;Come visit our learning environment, meet our team, and discover
what makes our curriculum unique. This is a wonderful opportunity to explore
our spaces, ask questions, and understand how we give a strong start to
every child.</p>
<p>&nbsp;To join us, please complete the registration form. Only one representative
per family/group is required to submit.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<strong>Register here: <a href="https://go.gov.sg/pzcxpb" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/pzcxpb</a> &nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<p>You may also scan the QR code on our poster.</p>
<p>&nbsp;See you at our Open House!</p>
