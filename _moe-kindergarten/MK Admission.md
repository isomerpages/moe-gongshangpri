<h4><strong>K1 &amp; K2 Admission 2026</strong></h4>
<p>Parents who are interested in enrolling your child to our MK, please submit
the waitlist application form via this link:</p>
<p><a href="https://go.gov.sg/mk2025waitlist" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/mk2025waitlist</a>
</p>
<p>We will contact you when a vacancy is available for your child.</p>
<h4><strong>K1 Admission 2027</strong></h4>
<p>Online registration for 2027 Kindergarten 1 (K1) admissions for both Open
Admissions (OA) and Early Years Centre (EYC) children will open from <strong>9am on Monday, 23 March 2026 and close at 4:30pm on Tuesday, 31 March 2026.</strong>
</p>
<p>Please register your child via the link below: <a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/preschool/moe-kindergarten/register/how-to-register" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.moe.gov.sg/preschool/moe-kindergarten/register/how-to-register</a>
